Frequently Asked Questions

We understand that security audits can raise many questions. Here are some of the most common ones:

  • How much does an audit cost?

    Every project is unique, and so is its audit. Pricing depends on factors like code complexity, project size, and risk level. Contact us for a personalized quote.

  • How long does an audit take?

    Timelines vary based on project complexity and scope, typically ranging from one week to several months. We provide an estimated duration along with our initial assessment.

  • When should I request an audit?

    The earlier, the better. You don’t need a finalized codebase — a work-in-progress version is enough for an initial security review. We also offer flexible reservations for projects still in development.

  • Do you verify fixes after the audit?

    Yes! We provide post-audit verification to ensure all identified vulnerabilities are properly addressed, strengthening your project’s security before deployment.

  • What happens if critical vulnerabilities are found?

    If we detect high-risk security flaws, we’ll provide a detailed report with remediation steps. We also offer direct collaboration with your development team to ensure fixes are properly implemented.

  • Do you offer re-audits?

    Yes, we provide follow-up audits if major changes are made to your smart contract after the initial audit. This ensures your security remains intact after upgrades.

  • Can I get a private audit report?

    By default, we provide confidential reports to clients. However, if you want to publish your audit for transparency, we can prepare a publicly shareable summary.

  • What blockchain networks do you support?

    We audit projects on Ethereum, Solana, Binance Smart Chain, Polygon, Avalanche, and more. If you’re working on a different chain, reach out—we likely support it too.
